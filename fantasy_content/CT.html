Cecilia Tallis

Cecilia is Briony's older sister, and is in love with Robbie. Over the course of the novel, Cecilia's numerous actions are witnessed by Briony and mistooken for assault. When Briony accuses Robbie of rape, Cecilia finds that her protests are ineffective at altering his fate, and that her family has already made its choice. Filled with resentment over how her family disregarded her complaints and turned on Robbie, Cecilia leaves to become a nurse.

Eventually, Cecilia is permanently reunited with Robbie after he manages to escape during the British evacuation at Dunkirk. While still estranged from her family and resentful towards Briony, she eventually meets Briony and reveals that Robbie has returned. After the war, it is implied that Cecilia was able to continue living with Robbie for the remainder of her life.
